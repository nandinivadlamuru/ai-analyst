# LabFox AI Program Overview

LabFox AI launched its analyst pilot in Q1 2025.
The main objective is to improve operational reporting quality while reducing manual analysis time.

The pilot team targets two regions initially:
- North
- West

The stated success metric is reducing report turnaround time by at least 25 percent by the end of 2025.

Phase-2 planning (for 2026) includes potential expansion to East after data quality gates are met.
The pilot cadence is a bi-weekly operating review and a monthly executive summary.

Operational KPIs tracked in this program:
- report turnaround days
- incident counts linked to data readiness
- variance between planned and actual spend
