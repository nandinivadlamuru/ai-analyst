# Governance and Policy Notes

The finance team approved a quarterly innovation budget of 120000 USD for the pilot.
Any monthly spend above 10000 USD requires leadership sign-off.

A cross-functional review must include at least one analyst and one operations manager.
Monthly close packages must be submitted by the third business day of the following month.
Any production incident with severity level P1 requires a post-incident review within 5 business days.

Approval matrix:
- Up to 10000 USD monthly spend: analyst manager approval
- Above 10000 USD monthly spend: leadership sign-off required
