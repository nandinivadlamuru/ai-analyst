# Risk Register Excerpt

Risk ID R-02 highlights potential delays caused by incomplete regional data feeds.
Mitigation for R-02: establish a weekly data-quality checkpoint and assign a single owner per region.

Risk ID R-05 tracks potential overrun of training resources during onboarding months.
Mitigation for R-05: stagger onboarding waves and cap training cohort size to 15 participants.

Risk ID R-07 covers dashboard adoption risk if reporting templates are not standardized.
Mitigation for R-07: publish a standard KPI dictionary and enforce template validation checks.
